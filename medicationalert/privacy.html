Privacy Policy - Medication Reminder

Last updated: June 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Medication Reminder ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal health information. All medication data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to:

  • Your medication names or schedules
  • Your dosage information
  • Your medical history
  • Any health-related notes you create

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

To improve the app's performance and user experience, we may collect anonymous usage data through third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app crashes (no personal data)

This data helps us understand how the app is used and identify technical issues.

4. Advertising

The free version of our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (city/country level)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings or by subscribing to our Premium version.

5. Premium Subscription

When you purchase a Premium subscription:

  • Payment is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • We only receive confirmation of your subscription status
  • We do not have access to your payment information

6. Data Storage and Security

All your medication data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any analytics data we collect.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Customize the app appearance with color themes (Premium feature)
  • Remove ads by subscribing to Premium
  • Request information about any data we may have about you

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@genuelabs.com

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Please contact us for more information about exercising these rights.

12. European Union Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, and erase your data.
