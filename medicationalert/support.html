Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medication?

Tap the + button on the home screen, enter your medication details, set the reminder times, and tap Save.

How do I set up reminders?

When adding a medication, you can set multiple reminder times. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

Can I track my medication history?

Yes! The app keeps a record of when you take or skip doses. You can view your medication history in the app.

How do I subscribe to Premium?

Tap the crown icon in the app or go to Settings > Premium Subscription to unlock ad-free experience and custom color themes.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your device's App Store settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your medication data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal health information on our servers.

What if I'm not receiving notifications?

Please check that notifications are enabled for Medication Reminder in your device settings. Also ensure Do Not Disturb mode is not blocking notifications.

The app doesn't remind me on specific days

Make sure you've selected the correct days when setting up your medication schedule. You can edit this in the medication settings.

How do I change the app theme?

Premium users can customize the app's color theme. Go to Settings > Theme Color and choose from various color options.

Can I change the app language?

Yes! Go to Settings > Language and select from English, Russian, Georgian, or German.
