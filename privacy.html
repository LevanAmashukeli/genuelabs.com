Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2025

Introduction

Genue Labs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across all our applications and services.

General Data Practices

We believe in minimal data collection. Our apps are designed to work primarily with local storage, keeping your personal information on your device whenever possible.

Application-Specific Privacy

Medication Reminder

Data Storage: All medication data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your medication names, schedules, or health information.

Analytics: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

Advertising: The free version displays ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and general location data.

Premium Subscription: Processed through App Store/Google Play. We only receive confirmation of subscription status.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of analytics collection where available
  • Request information about any data we may have
  • Export your data (in apps that support this feature)

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we process.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
