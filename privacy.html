Introduction

Genue Labs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across all our applications and services.

General Data Practices

We believe in minimal data collection. Our apps are designed to work primarily with local storage, keeping your personal information on your device whenever possible.

Application-Specific Privacy

Our Applications

Each of our applications has its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policy for each specific app you use:

In general, we follow these principles across all our applications:

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of analytics collection where available

Genue Labs is an independent software development company focused on creating useful mobile applications. We are not affiliated with any healthcare providers, pharmaceutical companies, or medical institutions.

Website Analytics

This website (genuelabs.com) may use analytics tools to understand visitor traffic and improve our site. This is separate from any data collection in our applications.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
